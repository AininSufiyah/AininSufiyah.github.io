Frequently Asked Questions

What does a review include?

OpusWrites provides professional services that help candidates present themselves in the best light possible to recruiters. We will review your resume to help you maximize job opportunities. Our experts will:
  • Provide confidential, personalized evaluation from trusted experts
  • feedback on layout, language and how well your resume communicates your skills and expertise
  • Personalized recommendations & edits on how to make your resume stronger

Does every job seeker need professional resume-writing services?

We don't offer professional resume writing services to everyone. That's why our resume experts will review your resume; to determine whether it's formatted correctly and successfully showcases your skills. If your resume is already in good shape, we will be sure to let you know!

How much does a professionally written resume cost?

We currently have two packages for students and field focused experts. Our resume-writing packages are flexible, allowing you to choose what you need regardless of your career level or reason for updating your resume.

How does the resume-writing process work?

OpusWrites uses the expertise of its resume writers and feedback from resume-scanning software called applicant tracking systems (ATS) to create the first draft of your resume. Once your writer has submitted the draft, you'll have one week to provide feedback after the first draft is delivered. After that, you'll have up to two rounds of revisions with your writer.

Does OpusWrites provide additional job-search services?

In addition to resumes, we professionally write cover letters and other formal letters upon request.

Does OpusWrites provide resume writing for specialized fields?

Yes, our resume writers have expert knowledge of the hiring process and work with you to create a resume that will appeal to ATS. This strategy allows us to customize your resume for each employer and industry, using keywords and formatting that will boost your chances of being selected for an interview.

How will you get my information?

We've designed our introductory questionnaire to help us get a sense of your past experience and current goals, as well as the most important factors in your job search. The questionnaire should take only a few minutes to complete, and your answers will allow us to customize your resume writing experience so that it saves you time and helps position you for success in today's competitive job market. While a previous resume is not required to start the process, it can save both you and your writer's time covering the basics.

How do you accept payments?

OpusWrites offers a number of long-term projects; therefore, we require an upfront payment to guarantee completion. Currently, OpusWrites only allows online banking payments through FPX Payment Gateway by Bizappay. Soon we aim to offer payment via credit card and e-wallet.
